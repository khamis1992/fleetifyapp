# FleetifyApp Quick Start Guide

## üéØ Get Started in 5 Minutes

Welcome to FleetifyApp! This guide will help you get up and running quickly with our fleet management system.

### Prerequisites
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- Internet connection
- Your company registration details (for new accounts)

## Step 1: Sign Up / Log In

### New Users
1. Go to [https://fleetifyapp.vercel.app](https://fleetifyapp.vercel.app)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top right corner
3. Fill in your information:
   - **Email**: Your work email address
   - **Password**: Create a strong password (min 8 characters)
   - **Company Name**: Your company name
   - **Phone Number**: Your contact number
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your email for verification (check spam folder if needed)
6. Click the verification link in the email
7. Log in with your credentials

### Existing Users
1. Go to [https://fleetifyapp.vercel.app](https://fleetifyapp.vercel.app)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Log In"**

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

1. After logging in, you'll see the profile completion screen
2. Fill in your company details:
   - **Company Address**: Your business address
   - **Phone Number**: Main company phone
   - **Business License**: Upload your business license
   - **Tax Information**: Add tax details for invoicing
3. Click **"Save Profile"**

## Step 3: Set Up Your First Vehicle

1. From the dashboard, click **"Fleet"** in the left menu
2. Click **"+ Add Vehicle"** button
3. Fill in vehicle details:
   ```
   Vehicle Information:
   - License Plate: ABC-1234
   - Make: Toyota
   - Model: Camry
   - Year: 2022
   - Vehicle Type: Sedan
   - Color: Silver

   Additional Details:
   - VIN: 1HGBH41JXMN109186
   - Engine Number: ABC123456
   - Registration Number: REG-2022-001
   - Insurance Policy: INS-2022-001
   ```
4. Click **"Save Vehicle"**

## Step 4: Create Your First Customer

1. Click **"Customers"** in the left menu
2. Click **"+ Add Customer"** button
3. Fill in customer information:
   ```
   Personal Information:
   - Name: John Doe
   - Email: john.doe@email.com
   - Phone: +1 234 567 8900
   - ID Number: 1234567890

   Address:
   - Street: 123 Main Street
   - City: New York
   - State: NY
   - ZIP Code: 10001
   ```
4. Click **"Save Customer"**

## Step 5: Create Your First Contract

1. Click **"Contracts"** in the left menu
2. Click **"+ Create Contract"** button
3. Fill in contract details:
   ```
   Contract Details:
   - Customer: John Doe (select from dropdown)
   - Vehicle: Toyota Camry - ABC-1234 (select from dropdown)
   - Start Date: Today's date
   - End Date: 30 days from start
   - Daily Rate: $50
   - Security Deposit: $500

   Terms:
   - Payment Terms: Monthly
   - Insurance Included: Yes
   - Maintenance Included: Yes
   ```
4. Click **"Create Contract"**

## Step 6: Process Your First Payment

1. From the contract details, click **"Record Payment"**
2. Fill in payment details:
   ```
   Payment Information:
   - Amount: $500 (security deposit)
   - Payment Method: Cash/Card/Bank Transfer
   - Payment Date: Today's date
   - Reference Number: PAY-2024-001
   ```
3. Click **"Record Payment"**
4. Print receipt if needed
